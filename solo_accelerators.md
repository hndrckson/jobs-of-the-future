# solo_accelerators.md

> Solo-oriented accelerators, builder programs, indie residencies, and
> self-directed research retreats for technical builders.
>
> Profile: EU national, BSc CS/AI/cyber, can work on own project or
> early-stage startup. Solo founders explicitly accepted.
>
> Priority: **equity-free or low-dilution + stipend/grant** > VC-backed
> accelerator with investment > prestige-only > unpaid.
> Filter: solo founders accepted, technical track, AI/ML/security adjacent.
