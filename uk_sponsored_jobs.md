# uk_sponsored_jobs.md

> UK AI / ML / cybersecurity / applied-research / agent infra roles for
> EU/non-UK nationals needing Skilled Worker visa sponsorship.
>
> Salary floors: £30k (new entrant rate), £38,700 (standard rate),
> **£41,700 (2026 Skilled Worker threshold)** — filter out anything below.
> Location priority: London > Cambridge > Manchester > Bristol/Edinburgh.
>
> Loop cadence: 48h. 🆕 = new since last scan. Drop entries >21 days old.
> Priority stack: **explicit sponsor + BSc ok** > implicit sponsor > no degree stated.
> Flag 🔥 = {Palantir, Darktrace, DeepMind, Anthropic} ∩ explicit-sponsor ∩ junior-ok.
>
> **Cross-check every org against the Home Office's [Register of Licensed Sponsors
> (Workers)](https://www.gov.uk/government/publications/register-of-licensed-sponsors-workers)**
> CSV before applying — saves time on dead ends. Register updated 2026-04-13.
